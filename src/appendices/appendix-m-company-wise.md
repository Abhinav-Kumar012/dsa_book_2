# Appendix M: Company-wise Preparation

Preparation strategies and commonly asked topics for top tech companies.

---

## General Tips for All Companies

1. **Research the company** — understand their products, culture, and recent news
2. **Know the interview format** — number of rounds, duration, focus areas
3. **Practice on the company's tagged problems** — LeetCode has company-specific problem lists
4. **Prepare behavioral stories** — most companies ask behavioral questions
5. **Practice system design** — for senior roles, system design is crucial
6. **Mock interviews** — practice with the company's style in mind

---

## Google

### Interview Style
- **Rounds:** 2 phone screens + 4-5 onsite (or virtual)
- **Focus:** Problem-solving ability, coding skills, Googleyness
- **Difficulty:** Medium to Hard
- **Unique:** Strong emphasis on clean code and edge cases

### Common Topics
- [ ] Arrays & Strings (very frequent)
- [ ] Trees & Graphs (very frequent)
- [ ] Dynamic Programming (frequent)
- [ ] System Design (for L5+)
- [ ] Recursion & Backtracking

### Frequently Asked Problems
- Longest Increasing Subsequence
- Number of Islands
- Course Schedule
- Merge Intervals
- Word Ladder
- Serialize/Deserialize Binary Tree
- Trapping Rain Water
- Alien Dictionary

### Tips
- Google loves **clean, readable code** — don't write hacky solutions
- **Test your code thoroughly** — Google interviewers expect you to find bugs
- **Discuss multiple approaches** — show your thought process
- **Ask clarifying questions** — Google interviewers appreciate this
- **Be prepared for follow-ups** — they often add constraints

- **Focus:** Hard problems, system design, behavioral

### Tier 2 Tech (Adobe, Atlassian, Salesforce, NVIDIA, Qualcomm)

- **Problems:** 150-200
- **Focus:** Medium problems, system design, company-specific

### Finance Tech (Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan, Visa)
- **Minimum:** 1-2 months of preparation
- **Problems:** 150-200
- **Focus:** Medium problems, finance knowledge, system design

### Other Tech (Walmart, Cisco, Intuit)
- **Minimum:** 1 month of preparation
- **Problems:** 100-150
- **Focus:** Medium problems, company-specific knowledge
