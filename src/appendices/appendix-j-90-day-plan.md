# Appendix J: 90-Day Study Plan

A comprehensive week-by-week plan to master all DSA topics. This plan assumes 3-4 hours of study per day.

---

## Overview

- **Duration:** 13 weeks (90 days)
- **Daily commitment:** 3-4 hours
- **Weekly pattern:** 5 days learning + 1 day practice + 1 day review
- **Total problems:** ~300-400
- **Goal:** Complete mastery of all major DSA topics

---
